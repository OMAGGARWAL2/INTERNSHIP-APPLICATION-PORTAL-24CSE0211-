Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account on InternHub?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details including your name, email, university information, and upload a profile photo. Once you verify your email, you'll be ready to start exploring internships.

How do I apply for an internship?

Browse through our internship listings, click on any position that interests you, and hit the "Apply Now" button. Make sure your profile is complete as employers will review your information when considering your application.

Can I track my application status?

Yes! Once logged in, you can view all your applications and their current status in your dashboard. You'll also receive notifications when there are updates to your applications.

What should I include in my profile?

Include a professional photo, your educational background, relevant skills, any previous experience, and a brief bio. The more complete your profile, the better your chances of getting noticed by employers.

I'm having trouble uploading my profile photo. What should I do?

Make sure your photo is in JPG, PNG, or GIF format and is less than 5MB in size. If you're still having issues, try using a different browser or clearing your browser cache.

How can my company post internship opportunities?

Companies can post internships by clicking "Post Internship" in the menu. You'll need to provide details about the position, requirements, and company information. All postings are reviewed before going live.

How do you verify companies on the platform?

We thoroughly verify all companies before they can post internships. This includes checking business registration, website verification, and reviewing company credentials to ensure legitimacy.

Is InternHub free to use for students?

Yes! InternHub is completely free for students. You can create a profile, browse internships, and apply to as many positions as you'd like without any charges.
